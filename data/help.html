Gurudeva App Features

Photos & Quotes

The main screen chooses a random photo from hundreds of pictures of Gurudeva taken over the years coupled with a quote. The quotes are randomly selected from the entire collection or from your favorites or from the category you have choosen (see below).

Next Photo/Quote and History

To get a new photo and quote selection (random), click on the right side of the quote where you see an arrow, or on the quote itself. If the quote is long you can scroll to read it. When you get to the end, these longer quotes require two taps or once on the arrow on the right, to get the next one. To view your last ten images and quotes, click on the left side of the quote. When you get to the tenth one and try to go back, the photo will move back and forth indicated that you are at the end of your recent history.

Move and Tap the Photo

You can use your finger to move the photo around and reveal more of the image. Tap the photo once to fit the whole image on screen. Tap a second time to view full size again.

Other Features

The tools at the bottom of the screen give you options related to the quotes and additional navigation.

Favorite Quotes

When you click favorites. You have two options: Add and View

Add: This adds the current quote to your favorite quotes file.

View: Will take you to the "reader" screen (which is where you are now) to read your favorite quotes.

Clear Favorites: When you are reading your favorites the icon on the bottom left changes to a Trash can. You can tap a quote to select it. When you click the trash can you can remove just that one selected quote from your favorites or you can delete all your favorites to start fresh.

Sharing

Click the above icon to invoke the sharing options.

Use the following buttons to capture the current quote that you are currently viewing on the main screen. If you are reading quotes on the text reader screen, tap any quote to select it first. Then you can send an email or tweet (a shorten version) to a friend.

Search

Use the search button to find a word or phrase in the quotes. The results are presented on the reader screen.

Categories

Using this tool at the bottom of the screen you can choose a category, a subset of all the quotes. Random selections from only this category will then appear with the photos. This resets to "All" each time the app is started. The icon shows gold bars to indicate that you are looking at only quotes from a single category. When you go back and click "All" the category bars turn back to gray.

On the screen with other options, when you click the "Categories" button, this just allows you to read all the the quotes from that one category, but it does not change the category of quotes that are showen on the main screen when you are viewing photos.

Other Options

This button takes you to the other options screen. From here you can read Gurudeva's biography, read, select or delete from your favorite quotes, read all quotes in any category, go to audio (see below,) read about this app and access this help info. If you click the "Explore" button, you will go to links that take you to our web sites in your web browser.

Audio Features

The app offers eight talks that are packaged and downloaded with the app. Internet access is not required to listen to these talks. The "Surprise Me" option requires internet access to choose a random talk from Gurudeva's archives on our web server. When you are on this screen, if you click the "share" icon, you will be sharing the location of that same audio on our web server. From there people can listen or download the audio. When starting a talk, the square black stop button appears to the left of the title. Click this to stop the playback.

If you want to download the audio: share the same with yourself (send to your own email address) and then go to the web page to download the audio.

Depending on your device settings, the audio should play continuously and you may move to other screens, let your phone sleep, etc. and the audio will still run. Disclaimer: this feature may vary as different device manufacturers may block continuous play.

If you move to another screen and audio is playing, a speaker icon appears in the top right corner. Clicking on this icon will take you back to the audio screen where you can stop the audio or choose a different one.
